What is The Passion Test?

The Passion Test is a simple to take, fun to do, S-Y-S-T-E-M (Save Yourself Time, Energy and Money)

The Passion Test SYSTEM is unbelievably easy to follow and can provide the most profound changes in you life you may have ever experienced.

Developed by NY Times best selling authors Janet Bray and Chris Attwood - this process gives you the tools to discover and align with your Top 5 Passions to put yourself on you own personal path to creating more of what you want in your life.

Seriously we should, because I can almost certainly help and probably a lot quicker than you imagine.

The Passion Test will help you:

Become CLEAR about what matters most to you in your life

Learn the ONE SECRET to living a passionate life

Discover your life purpose

Create more balance in your life

Attract more abundance in every area of your life

Use your passions as a barometer for making choices and decisions.

How you can take The Passion Test:

Personal Consultation Personalized one-on-one course designed for you

Book or Join a Workshop Join other local Passion-Seekers

Host a Passion Party and take the Passion Test with friends & family

ONE-ON-ONE Sessions Two 1-hour Private one-on-one sessions

Chris and Janet have spent more than 30 years studying what it takes to be successful in life. They sought out people who are living their lives to the max, manifesting their dreams, creating whatever it takes to be completely fulfilled. All of those interviewed agreed that their success had come from learning to identify and follow their Passions.

The idea of living your passions in not new, but Janet and Chris have developed a SYSTEM that anyone can easily learn. The Passion Test process also introduces self-directed tools to remove any false beliefs, concepts or ideas that may be holding us back from moving in the direction of our Passions.

Larry Tucker is a Certified Passion Test Facilitator, who has been personally trained by Chris and Janet Attwood, authors of the best-selling book The Passion Test: The Effortless Path to Discovering Your Purpose to teach their 3-hour group workshop, and to take participants through two individual, 1-hour session, One-on-One course.
